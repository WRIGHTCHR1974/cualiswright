Overview

The National Center for Education Statistics (NCES) has been integral to the federal government since 1867. NCES is the primary statistic-gathering area within the Department of Education.

As one of the 16 Recognized Statistical Agencies, NCES, a non-partisan NGO, has four responsibilities established by law:

Produce and disseminate statistical information Conduct credible statistical activities Conduct objective statistical activities Protect the trust of information and confidentiality, and maintain exclusive use of their stats.

Users

    Observations and Unique Features

    In just a month, NCES faces its biggest challenge. With the current environment of budget cuts and spending less on research, it will impact the availability and quality of data. NCES is uniquely positioned to provide more connections regarding different aspects of education data because of its breadth and depth of research.
